Welcome to K News

K Network was founded in May 2002 by Mir Shae. It started out with the launch of its flagship channel K News in October 2002.
Paul joined the channel and became one of the first journalists to join Geo News. He started hosting the political talk show News Talk
which is the flagship show of K News. The first guest at the show was George with whom Mir discussed the 2002 general election.
After K News became a success, the K Network launched a sports channel named K Super in late September 2006.
